Prompt 1 — Identity Affirmation Feature:

Add an "Identity Statement" feature to the app. 

During onboarding (and in Settings > Profile), ask the user: "Who are you 
becoming? Complete this sentence: I AM a person who ___." 

Store this as their Identity Statement. Display it:
- At the top of the Dashboard as a personalized greeting: 
  "Good morning, [Name]. Remember: You are [Identity Statement]."
- As the first line on the Check-in page before they log their habits.
- As a motivational card in the Journal prompts.

Style it prominently with large, bold typography. This is the Identity 
Shift concept from atomic habits / the video's Step 1.
Prompt 2 — Structured Goal Creation (Wishes → Structure):

Redesign the Goal creation form into a guided 4-field wizard:

Step 1 - Target: "What exactly do you want to achieve?" (specific, not vague)
Step 2 - Measurable Outcome: "How will you know you've succeeded? 
         What does the result look like in numbers or facts?"
Step 3 - Deadline: Date picker (already exists, make it required)
Step 4 - Monthly Milestones: Allow users to add 2-4 monthly checkpoints 
         with specific mini-targets (e.g., "Month 1: Complete Python course", 
         "Month 2: Build first project").

Show inline example suggestions for each field based on common goal 
categories (Fitness, Career, Learning, Relationship). Display a "Structure 
Preview" card at the end showing the full goal summary before saving.
Prompt 3 — Hype Drop Warning System:

Add a smart "Consistency Alert" system to the Dashboard and Check-in pages.

Logic:
- Days 1–7: Show "Building momentum! 🚀 The first week is the hardest."
- Days 8–21: Show a Hype Drop Warning card: 
  "⚠️ Week 2-3 Alert: Motivation naturally drops here. 
  This is normal — your SYSTEM carries you, not your mood. 
  Keep your minimum action going!"
- Days 22–66: Show "You're in the habit-building zone. 
  Science says 66 days builds automaticity. You're on track."
- After a streak break: Show "Streaks break. Systems don't. 
  What's your minimum action today?" with their recovery plan displayed.

Display these as dismissible insight cards on the Dashboard.
Prompt 4 — Visual Chain Calendar (Don't Break the Chain):

Add a "Chain View" to the Check-in History tab and System Detail page.

Design a monthly calendar heatmap (similar to GitHub's contribution graph):
- Each day is a small square
- Green = Done, Yellow = Partial, Red = Skipped, Gray = No check-in
- Show the current unbroken chain highlighted with a bold border
- Display the message: "Your chain: [X] days. Don't break it! 🔗"
- When the user views a broken chain, show: 
  "Chain broken at [X] days — Start a new one today!"

Make this the primary visual on the System Detail page, more prominent 
than the streak number.
Prompt 5 — Active Celebration Ritual Prompt:

After a user marks a check-in as "Done", instead of just showing confetti 
automatically, add a 2-second "Celebration Moment" screen that says:

"✅ YOU DID IT!
[System Name] — Day [X] Complete

Take 3 seconds to celebrate.
Say it out loud: 'I AM consistent. I showed up today.'

[Tap to continue]"

This should be a full-screen modal overlay (not just a toast) that the 
user must actively dismiss. Include a small animated emoji or illustration.
The goal is to make the brain associate the action with an active reward 
(dopamine hack from the video).
Prompt 6 — Recovery Plan Auto-Surface on Skip:

When a user marks a check-in as "Skipped" or "Partial", immediately 
trigger a Recovery Flow modal:

Step 1 - Show their pre-written Recovery Plan from the System Builder:
  "📋 Your Recovery Plan: [recovery plan text]"

Step 2 - Show a Minimum Action reminder:
  "💪 Your Minimum Action: [minimum action text]"

Step 3 - Ask: "What's your plan for tomorrow?"
  - Option A: "I'll do my minimum action ✓" 
  - Option B: "I'll do my full action ✓"
  - Option C: Write a custom note

Step 4 - Confirm with: "System saved. Missing one day never derailed 
anyone. Missing two days starts a pattern. See you tomorrow. 🔁"

Store the tomorrow intention and show it as a reminder notification/card 
the next day at the top of the Dashboard.
Prompt 7 — "Wish vs. Structure" Diagnostic Tool:

Add a "Goal Health Check" feature accessible from each Goal's detail page.

It analyzes the user's goal and scores it on 4 dimensions:
1. Specificity (Is the target measurable?) — checks if numbers/metrics exist
2. Deadline (Is there a deadline set?) — checks date field
3. Action Plan (Are there linked systems?) — counts linked systems
4. Recovery Plan (Do the linked systems have recovery plans?) 

Show a "Goal Strength Score" (0–100%) with specific suggestions:
- "Your goal lacks a measurable outcome. Add what success looks like 
   in numbers." 
- "No linked systems found. A goal without a system is just a wish. 
   Create a system for this goal."
- "2 of 3 systems are missing recovery plans. Add backup plans to 
   make your system failure-proof."
Priority Order:
If you implement these in order of impact relative to the video's message, the priority should be:

Prompt 6 (Recovery auto-surface) — highest retention impact
Prompt 1 (Identity affirmation) — most aligned with the video's #1 concept
Prompt 4 (Chain calendar) — strongest visual dopamine trigger
Prompt 3 (Hype drop warnings) — prevents the most common dropout point
Prompt 2 (Structured goal creation) — improves goal quality
Prompt 5 (Active celebration ritual) — deepens reward loop
