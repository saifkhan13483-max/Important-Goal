5. SYSTEM BUILDER UX REDESIGN
This is the most important area.

Redesign the 7-step system builder wizard to feel like a guided coach for beginners.
Each step should:
- have a simple title
- have a one-line explanation
- show an example
- explain why this matters
- avoid jargon

For each step:
Step 1: Goal
- Ask what the user wants to achieve
- Explain that goals give direction

Step 2: Identity
- Ask who they want to become
- Example: “I am someone who works out consistently”

Step 3: Trigger
- Ask when/where the habit will happen
- Example: “After brushing my teeth…”

Step 4: Minimum Action
- Ask for the smallest possible action
- Example: “Do 5 pushups”

Step 5: Reward
- Ask how they will acknowledge success immediately
- Example: “Mark it complete and say ‘done’”

Step 6: Backup Plan
- Ask what they will do on difficult days
- Example: “Do just 1 pushup”

Step 7: Review
- Show full system summary in a beautiful card
- Make it feel satisfying and clear before saving

Also:
- Add progress indicator
- Add tips panel
- Add example chips users can click
- Add a “Keep it simple” reminder
- Make the form conversational and visually light
