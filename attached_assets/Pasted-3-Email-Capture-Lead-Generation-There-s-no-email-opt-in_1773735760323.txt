3. Email Capture / Lead Generation
There's no email opt-in — no newsletter signup, no "Get early access" form, no exit-intent capture. Every visitor who leaves without signing up is lost forever. An email list is the most durable growth asset you can build.


4. Functional Payment Gates (Feature Limiting)
Even though tiers are defined (e.g., "Free = 2 goals, 3 systems"), there's no enforcement in the app. Any user can access all features regardless of plan. This makes paid tiers meaningless and kills upgrade motivation.


Tier 2 — High Impact (Conversion & Trust)
5. Real Testimonials with Photos & Names
The hero says "Trusted by 12,000+ people" but there are no named quotes, avatars, or verifiable social proof beneath that claim. Specific testimonials with faces dramatically increase conversion — generic trust badges alone do not.


6. Cookie Consent / GDPR Banner
There is no cookie consent mechanism. This is a legal requirement in the EU and increasingly expected globally, especially for a product that uses Firebase (which sets cookies). Non-compliance is a liability.


7. Interactive Product Demo / Video
The "See How It Works" CTA scrolls to a steps section, but there's no actual product video or interactive preview. Users should be able to see the check-in flow, system builder, or dashboard before signing up — this is one of the highest-leverage conversion tools for SaaS.


8. Forgot Password / Auth Error Handling
A forgot-password.tsx page exists but needs to be verified as actually functional end-to-end. Auth failures that drop users silently are one of the top causes of signup abandonment.


Tier 3 — Strong UX & Retention Improvements
9. Onboarding Completion Checklist
After signup, new users land in the app with no clear "next step." A simple onboarding checklist ("Create your first goal → Build a system → Complete your first check-in") dramatically reduces early churn by giving users a win within the first session.


10. Email Notifications / Drip Sequence
Push notifications exist in concept, but there are no transactional emails (welcome email, streak reminders, weekly digest). Email is far more reliable for re-engagement than browser push, especially on desktop.


11. Structured Data (JSON-LD)
Adding schema markup (WebApplication, FAQPage, Review types) to the landing page can earn rich snippets in Google — ratings, FAQs, and app information displayed directly in search results. This is a free traffic amplifier.


12. Referral / Share-Your-Streak Feature
There's no way for users to share their streak or system publicly. A "Share my 30-day streak" card (image or link) is a zero-cost viral loop — it brings in qualified users who see a friend succeeding.


Tier 4 — Performance & Technical Debt
13. Image & Asset Optimization
No evidence of lazy-loaded images, WebP conversion, or a CDN for static assets. Landing page load speed directly affects both SEO rankings (Core Web Vitals) and bounce rate.


14. PWA / Installable App
The app is mobile-first in design but not installable. Adding a Web App Manifest and service worker would allow users to "install" SystemForge on their home screen, improving daily check-in rates significantly.


15. Analytics Event Tracking
There's an analytics.service.ts but no clear integration with a product analytics tool (Mixpanel, PostHog, or even GA4 events). Without funnel tracking, you cannot tell where users drop off during onboarding or which features drive retention.


16. Error Boundary & 404 Handling
A not-found.tsx exists, but there's no global React error boundary to gracefully catch runtime crashes. An unhandled error currently likely shows a blank white screen, which destroys trust.
